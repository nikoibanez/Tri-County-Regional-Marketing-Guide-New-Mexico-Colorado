## Publication Notes

Public-facing language should continue to say “submit,” “contact,” “ask,” or “verify fit.” Do not claim guaranteed posting, free placement, audience size, ad availability, or event acceptance unless the source page explicitly says so.

Social pages and Facebook groups are useful outreach leads, but they are not formal public-notice routes and should not be presented as guaranteed distribution.
