## LocalStash / Weekender Clarification

LocalStash is still a useful regional amplifier lead for Southern Colorado and Northern New Mexico. The best evidence found in this pass indicates:

- The former Weekender page now points users to LocalStash.
- LocalStash/Weekender posts say they remain committed to regional events.
- Their workflow appears to rely heavily on public Facebook Events.
- Older Weekender posts referenced an event submission form, but the currently reliable public site route is LocalStash contact plus public Facebook Event creation.

Do not describe LocalStash as a guaranteed event calendar. Better public copy:

> Create a public Facebook Event first, then contact LocalStash if the event has Southern Colorado or Northern New Mexico regional appeal.
